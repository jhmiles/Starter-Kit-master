THE BEGINNING

Amerikan Made Prints has been a small screen printing studio for a couple of years now, and it consists of myself and my wife, Jenn. Together, our goal is to turn out hand-printed posters that rock harder than Gibraltar. Our first year was pretty successful as we consistently sold out of each new posters run, some posters we had to do second and third printings of to meet the demand.

THE DETOUR

During our second year, just as we were hitting a great stride, we decided to take a hiatus to concentrate our attention on our biggest project: the birth of our son. It’s now been 4 years since his birth and almost two years on hiatus. The urge to return to the life of laying down ink is too strong to ignore. It’s a part of our life that we are very passionate about and it has sorely been missed.
